## Amazon FBA: End-to-End Ready

### Packaging That Passes Amazon Receiving
Amazon FBA inbound requirements are strict. Packages that fail inspection are rejected, incurring costly returns and storage fees. **Walyield** has developed FBA-specific packaging protocols:

- **Drop-tested outer cartons**: 5-layer corrugated boxes rated for the mechanical stress of Amazon's automated handling systems
- **Suffocation warning labels**: Applied to poly-bagged items as required by Amazon policy
- **FBA carton labels**: Printed and applied per Amazon specifications (2 labels per carton, proper placement)
- **Pallet configuration**: 48" × 40" GMA-standard pallets, stretch-wrapped, with proper pallet labels

### The FBA-Ready Workflow
1. **Design & Sampling**: Product design confirmed, pre-production sample approved
2. **Production**: Mass production with in-line QC (four-stage protocol)
3. **FBA Prep**: Labeling, bagging, carton marking, palletizing
4. **Shipment**: Sea/Air freight from Port of Shanghai → FBA warehouse (US/UK/DE/AU/JP)
5. **Delivery**: Direct to Amazon fulfillment center, ready for Prime availability
